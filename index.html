Travel Tips!

Pack light by choosing versatile clothing that can be mixed and matched.

Set a daily budget and stick to local eateries to save money.

Visit popular photo spots early in the morning to avoid crowds.

Plan group activities ahead to avoid last-minute disagreements.

Traveling solo? Always share your itinerary with a friend or family member.

Research lesser-known spots for a more peaceful and unique experience.

Set expectations and roles with friends before the trip starts.

Roll your clothes instead of folding to save suitcase space.
