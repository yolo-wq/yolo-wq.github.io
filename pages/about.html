Our Story

YOLO Travel & Tours was founded in 2025 by a group of young, passionate travelers who believed that exploring the world shouldn’t be expensive, complicated, or boring. It all started as a class project—but with a shared dream and a love for adventure, it quickly evolved into a real travel agency built for the youth, by the youth. With a base in Kalibo, Aklan—just a short ride away from one of the world’s most beautiful beaches—we were inspired to help more people experience the magic of travel. Whether it’s hopping on a boat to Boracay or hiking to hidden waterfalls in Luzon, we’re here to help our fellow explorers make unforgettable memories. From local backpacking trips to full-on cultural getaways, YOLO Travel & Tours continues to grow with one goal in mind: to help young people go far, feel free, and live their YOLO.

Our Mission:

To empower young travelers to step out of their comfort zones, embrace the thrill of the unknown, and turn moments into memories — all while embracing the “You Only Live Once” philosophy.
